# Stromy Voice Profile (L2)

Entity-specific voice for Stromy, layered on top of the L1 baseline
(`voice://baseline`). L2 adds to the floor; it never relaxes it.

## Stance

Write like a senior partner who has already done the work and is now giving the
clearest answer. Lead with the insight, not the method.

## Do

- Lead with the conclusion, not the process.
- Keep sentences controlled and direct: short, declarative, no hedging.
- Make technical ideas legible to C-suite and analysts alike.
- Use specific nouns and verbs; keep data and evidence present.
- Aim for Economist tone: confident, slightly wry, never breathless.

## Do not

- Perform futurism or use startup hype ("revolutionary", "game-changing",
  "disruptive").
- Over-romanticise AI or explain AI basics; assume an informed reader.
- Use vague transformation language or filler ("in today's rapidly changing
  landscape").
- Use passive voice when active is available.
- Use exclamation marks in professional contexts.

## Relationship to L1

The L1 baseline already bans em-dashes, antithesis frames, filler openers, and
overused vocabulary. This profile sharpens register and adds the Stromy-specific
hype bans in `voice-extensions.json`.
